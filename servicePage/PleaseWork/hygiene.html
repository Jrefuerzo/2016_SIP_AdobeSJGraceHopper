City Team

Many of the homeless that come do not have the resources or money to purchase hygiene products. Being able to shave, brush their teeth, and get cleaned-up is important for increased self-esteem but also for good health. For many men, women and children – these products are a luxury. A Hygiene Drive is a great way to help someone gain their confidence back and begin the path to a restored life.

Tampon/Pad Drive

Feminine hygiene products are essential. However, they are rarely donated to homeless shelters. It is, perhaps, due to lack of conversation that feminine hygiene products are not frequently donated to homeless shelters. With your generous donations, we plan to raise money and awareness for the health of homeless women.

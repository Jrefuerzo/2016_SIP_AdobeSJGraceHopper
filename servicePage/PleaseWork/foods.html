Move for Hunger

Move for Hunger is a non-profit organization that offers to pick up unwanted, non-perishable food items from those who are moving and deliver it to their local food banks. Working across 60 states, Move for Hunger has delivered over 6,029,990 pounds of food across North America. Help this organization but hosting a drive at your school.

Second Harvest Food Bank

Based in Silicon Valley, Second Harvest is one of the largest food banks in the nation. Every month, Second Harvest provides more than one quarter of a million people with nutritious food and fresh produce. In one year, more than one million pounds of nutritious food each week was provided to low-income people in need in every zip code. This would not be possible without the 1,500 volunteers fundraisers who collect food and raise money.

SF-Marin Food Bank & Fund Drives

Thanks to our thousands of donors and volunteers, 47 million pounds of food have been delivered throughout San Francisco and Marin. Start a drive now and start filling up your food collection barrel. All food donated goes directly into the community. By hosting a fund drive you can raise money that would will directly support the program. For every $1 donated, the food bank is able to distribute $5 worth of food.

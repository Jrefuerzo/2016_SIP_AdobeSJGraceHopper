Serve Hot Meals

Loaves and Fishes

Monday through Friday from 9AM-4PM

Our volunteer opportunities are open every Monday - Friday. As a volunteer, you will be asked to prep fruits and vegetables, serve hot meals, pour beverages, wash dishes, and tidy-up the site facility. Please be 14 years of age, or over (unless accompanied by a parent or guardian). Happy volunteering!

Distribute Food in the Community

Second Harvest Food Bank

Every third Saturday from 9AM- 12PM

Volunteers are needed to assist with the Food Bank's direct service food distribution sites throughout Santa Clara and San Mateo Counties assisting with food distribution, membership renewals, clerical and more. These positions are for volunteers who are able to commit to helping every week at our Brown Bag sites or once a month at our Family Harvest sites.
